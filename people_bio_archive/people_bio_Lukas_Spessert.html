Lukas Spessert

M.S. 2020 Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich, Germany
lukas.spessert at berkeley.edu

Lukas was born and raised in Munich, Germany. At Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich he conducted his bachelor thesis in the group of Prof. Dirk Trauner working on the total synthesis of cytochalasan natural products. After that he joined the group of Prof. Paul Knochel to synthesize ant pheromones using chiral secondary alkylcopper reagents, before going for an internship in the medicinal chemistry department of Boehringer Ingelheim. There he was focusing on the synthesis of new potential drug candidates in the fields of immunology and respiratory diseases. Driven by his passion for total synthesis, he joined the Maimone Lab to learn more about the beauty of terpenes. Outside of lab, Lukas enjoys hitting the gym, playing computer games and trying all the great food the Bay has to offer.
