Alvin Hsu

expected B.S. 2019 University of California, Berkeley
alvin.hsu at berkeley.edu

Alvin was born and raised in Fremont, CA. He was introduced to science at a young age, and quickly fell in love with chemistry. After taking organic chemistry and enjoying the problem-solving aspects of synthesis, he decided to join the Maimone group during the spring of 2016 to further improve his skills in organic chemistry. During his spare time, Alvin enjoys playing the piano, going out with friends, and exploring the restaurants of Berkeley.
