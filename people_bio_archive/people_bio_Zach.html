Zach Brill

2012 B.A. Columbia University
zach.brill at berkeley.edu

Zach was born and raised in the great state of New Jersey, where he was exposed to chemicals at an early age. He then moved to the big city, attending Columbia University and graduated with a B.A. in Chemistry in 2012. While at Columbia, Zach worked for Prof. Scott Snyder, completing the total synthesis of the resveratrol-derived natural products caraphenols B and C, and working towards the synthesis of halogenated sesquiterpenoids. Though a New-Yorker at heart, he decided to make the cross-country trip to UC Berkeley in 2012. Zach greatly enjoys exploring, both in the lab and the outside world. He has played the violin for most of his life, and loves discovering new music during long hours in lab.
